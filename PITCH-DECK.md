**The Challenge:**

- Universities lack updated Tech & AI curriculum

**The Impact:**
- Talented youth miss opportunities in the $5 trillion global tech economy
- Universities struggle to provide relevant, up-to-date tech education
- Companies can't find qualified local developers
- Brain drain as skilled workers leave for better opportunities

**The Barrier:**
Cost is the #1 barrier preventing African youth from accessing quality tech education.
